Worldview Windows:

Myhths for Bangladesh, Brazil and Mexico

Worldview Windows is a collection of myths from Bangladesh, Brazil and Mexico, featuring the way these cultures view the world and exhibiting the close relationship these peoples cultivate with nature, particularly with the land and water.

The audiobook contents are as follows:

Foreword

Bonbibi, from Bangladesh

The pink river dolphin, from Brazil

The mermaid of Metepec, from Mexico
